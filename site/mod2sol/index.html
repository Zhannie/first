To make the pastry, combine the flour, baking powder and 1 teaspoon of sea salt in a large bowl. Finely chop or coarsely grate the butter, then rub into the dry mix until it resembles fine breadcrumbs. Mix in just enough cold water to bring it together, then wrap in sclingfilm and pop in the fridge for 1 hour.

Sushi

To make a California roll, cover a sheet of nori with the sushi rice, sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds, then carefully turn the sheet of nori and rice over onto the sushi mat so the rice is on the bottom and nori on top. Add any combination of fillings, roll and chop as before.
